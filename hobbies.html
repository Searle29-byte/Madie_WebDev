Life Outside the Sales Floor

I believe the same grit that gets you through mile 80 of a century ride gets you through a rough quarter. Here's what keeps me sharp.

Endurance & Athletics

Built to go the distance

I've completed the 100-mile St. George Century Bike Race and the Colorado Springs Marathon. Endurance sports taught me something sales never fully can: that discomfort is temporary, and showing up consistently is the only strategy that works long-term.

I grew up in Franktown, Colorado - a small town east of Denver where the Rockies are close enough to feel personal. Getting outside has never been optional for me. It's how I reset.


My regular rotation of activities:

  1. Cycling - road and trail, any distance
  2. Running - training year-round
  3. Hiking - especially anything with elevation gain
  4. Pickleball - competitive and improving fast
  5. Swing dancing - yes, really
